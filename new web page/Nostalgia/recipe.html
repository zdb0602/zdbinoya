Kaldereta

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef cubed
  • 3 pieces garlic cloves crushed and chopped
  • 1 piece onion finely chopped
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 piece red bell pepper sliced
  • 1 piece green bell pepper sliced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup liver spread processed using blender
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 2 cups potatoes sliced
  • 2 cups carrots sliced
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 2/3 cup green olives
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the cooking oil in the cooking pot or pressure cooker.
    1/4 cup cooking oil
  2. Sauté the onion and garlic.
    1 piece onion, 3 pieces garlic cloves
  3. Add the beef. Cook for 5 minutes or until the color turns light brown.
    2 lbs beef
  4. Add the dried bay leaves and chili flakes or crushed pepper. Stir.
    3 pieces dried bay leaves, 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  5. Add the liver spread. Stir.
    ½ cup liver spread
  6. Pour-in the tomato sauce and beef broth.
    1 cup tomato sauce, 2 cups beef broth
  7. Cook the beef until it becomes tender
    (about 30 mins if using a pressure cooker, or 1 to 2 hours if using an ordinary pot).
  8. Add potato and carrots. Cook for 8 to 10 minutes.
    2 cups potatoes, 2 cups carrots
  9. Put the green olives and bell peppers in the cooking pot.
    Stir and continue to cook for 5 minutes more.
    1 piece red bell pepper, ⅔ cup green olives, 1 piece green bell pepper
  10. Put the green olives and bell peppers in the cooking pot.
    Stir and continue to cook for 5 minutes more.
    1 piece red bell pepper, ⅔ cup green olives, 1 piece green bell pepper
  11. Add salt and pepper to taste
  12. Serve Hot. Enjoy!
